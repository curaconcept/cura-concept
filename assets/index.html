Frequently Asked Questions

How is the hosting actually free?

Instead of renting space on a traditional server that requires constant upkeep, we compile your website into pure code assets. We then deploy these directly to global Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) which host static files entirely for free.

What if I need to update my content?

We can integrate a "Headless CMS" (Content Management System) for you. You log into a clean dashboard to edit text or images, and the moment you hit publish, our engine automatically re-compiles your site and pushes the update to the free edge network.

Do you work with existing websites?

Yes. We specialize in auditing heavy WordPress or Wix sites, extracting their design and content, and completely rewriting the underlying architecture to eliminate your hosting fees.
